Welcome to the Wesnoth Era Randomizer Program!
Click the "One-Click Build" button to instantly build an add-on which adds a randomized multiplayer era to Battle for Wesnoth.

To install: Extract the zip and move the newly extracted directory into your Wesnoth add-ons directory.

Where to find the Wesnoth add-ons directory
Windows: C:\Users\G\Documents\My Games\Wesnoth<version>\data\add-ons
macOS: ~/Library/Containers/org.wesnoth.Wesnoth/Data/Library/Application Support/Wesnoth_<version>/data/add-ons
Linux: ~/.local/share/wesnoth/<version>/data/add-ons
Linux (installed via flatpak): ~/.var/app/org.wesnoth.Wesnoth/data/wesnoth/<version>/data/add-ons
